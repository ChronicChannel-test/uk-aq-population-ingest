# PCON boundaries by year (population series)

All series are mid-year population estimates. Use the boundary vintage shown for each year range to keep geography and population aligned.

| Year | E+W | S | NI |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2001-2010 | No E+W PCON population series in Nomis (pre-2011) | NRS PCON 2005 boundaries (2001 onward) | NISRA PCON historical series (mid-2001 to mid-2020) |
| 2011-2020 | Nomis NM_2010_1 / TYPE460 (PCON Dec 2010 boundaries) | NRS PCON 2005 boundaries (2001 onward) | NISRA PCON historical series (mid-2001 to mid-2020) |
| 2021-2024 | Nomis NM_2014_1 / TYPE172 (PCON Jul 2024 boundaries) | NRS PCON 2005 boundaries (2001 onward) | NISRA PCON 2024 series (2021-2024) |

Notes:
- Northern Ireland also publishes a PCON 2008 series for 2021-2024. Pick one vintage and stick with it for that range.
- England + Wales PCON population series in Nomis starts at mid-2011 for these boundary sets.
